Meet friends, have fun, let’s fly...

If you are looking for a flying club near Nampa, Idaho Top Fun Flyers is a good place to meet new friends, eat good food, listen to interesting speakers and learn new things. All based on our love of all things flying.
Weekly:
Our flying club meets for breakfast every Saturday morning, 8:30 am, at the Tower Grill at the Nampa Airport, and then decides if we’re going to fly anywhere. Top Fun Flyers flying club is a group of pilots located in Treasure Valley Idaho, based at the Nampa, Idaho Airport (KMAN). We are a flying club in Idaho serving Caldwell, Nampa, Meridian, Eagle, Kuna, Melba, Boise as well as many other surrounding communities. MONTHY MEETINGS: We meet the last Thursday of each month except December, in Sheprock hangar at the Nampa Airport. Come at 6:30 pm, business starts at 7:00 pm. Bring potluck food if you plan to eat with us. Address: 121 Municipal Dr, Nampa, ID 83687
